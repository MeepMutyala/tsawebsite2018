Amrai

A new revolutionary tool advancing the future of business, education, and technology.

AZCA Technologies is a company that specializes in technological innovation. Our mission is to implement artificial intelligence in technology, increasing the capabilities of technology to aid the human world. One of the major projects we have been working on is Amrai (Applicable Machine Robot Artificial Intelligence), a robot that aims to provide assistance in both business and education fields. We have identified the tedious tasks students, teachers, and office workers can use assistance in and designed Amrai as a solution to them. Amrai utilizes artificial intelligence to act as a mobile printer, deliver materials and paper, help format documents, place calls, and grade short responses.

Designed to help you.

Amrai was created by AZCA Technologies after identifying and assessing problems, difficulties, and tedious tasks students, teachers, and business workers face in today's society. Amrai will be able to assist in several tasks, including, but not limited to:

  • Printing papers and distributing them to students or peers
  • Making calls and scheduling meetings
  • Organizing and formatting files
  • Grading short responses
  • Acting as a substitute teacher or assistant

Who are we?

Our product is a solution to a design brief presented to our team in Technology Student Association, and our team is part of MOT Charter High School's TSA Chapter. MOT Charter High is a school that includes both a STEM academy and an Arts academy that are designed to help students find their interests and reach their career goals. In the STEM academy, students can choose to major in Engineering, Computer Science, or Biotechnology
